Beach Safety

While the beach can be a fun and relaxing place, it's important to stay safe. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Always swim in designated areas that are supervised by lifeguards
  • Don't swim alone, regardless of your age or level of swimming skills
  • Keep an eye on the tide
  • Protect yourself from the sun
  • Stay hydrated
  • Check the weather forecast before you head out
  • Be aware of your surroundings
  • Watch out for wildlife
